# 贡献者公约 / Code of Conduct

> 本项目采用 [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) 的简化中英双语版本。
>
> This project adopts a simplified bilingual (Chinese/English) version of the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

---

## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

我们作为成员、贡献者、维护者承诺，让参与本项目的每一个人都不受骚扰和歧视，无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、族裔、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向。

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

我们承诺以促进开放、包容、多元、健康社区的方式行动和互动。

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## 我们的准则 / Our Standards

**正面行为示例 / Examples of positive behavior**:

- 对他人展示同理心和善意 / Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- 尊重不同意见、观点和经验 / Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- 给予建设性反馈，也优雅地接受反馈 / Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- 承担责任，向受我们错误影响的人道歉，并从中学习 / Accepting responsibility, apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience
- 关注的不仅是个人，更是整个社区 / Focusing on what is best for the overall community

**不可接受行为示例 / Examples of unacceptable behavior**:

- 使用性化语言或意象、性关注或挑逗 / The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- 钓鱼、侮辱或贬损的评论，人身或政治攻击 / Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- 公开或私下骚扰他人 / Public or private harassment
- 未经明确许可发布他人私人信息（如物理或电子地址） / Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission
- 其他可被合理认定为不专业或不当的行为 / Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## 执行责任 / Enforcement Responsibilities

社区维护者有责任澄清和执行本公约的行为标准，并对他们认为不当、威胁、冒犯或有害的任何行为采取适当且公平的纠正措施。

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

---

## 适用范围 / Enforcement Scope

本公约适用于所有项目空间，以及在公共空间中个人代表项目或社区时的行为。

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

---

## 执行 / Enforcement

若发现不可接受的行为，可通过 GitHub Issues 私密联系维护者（请勿在公开 Issue 中讨论违规行为本身）。

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers responsible for enforcement via GitHub Issues (please do not discuss the violation itself in a public issue).

所有投诉都会被及时、公正地审查和处理。所有报告者都会收到回复。

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All complaints will receive a reply.
